The Flying Scotsman

The Flying Scotsman, built in 1923, quickly gained fame for being the first steam locomotive to officially reach 100 mph and for its record-setting speed between London and Edinburgh. Designed to be both powerful and fast, this iconic train represented British engineering prowess during the early 20th century. Over the years, it became a national treasure, celebrated for its elegance, historical significance, and adventurous legacy.
